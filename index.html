The Artist

The Future
"King of Melodies"

The future "King of Melodies," Moqemàe is a multifaceted artist hailing from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. A distinguished singer, rapper, and songwriter, Moqemàe has earned acclaim as an award-winning artist and captivating performer. His musical journey transcends traditional genres, weaving a rich soundscape that reflects various moods.

Musical Roots

Moqemàe grew up in a music-oriented family, where music was playing around the house 24/7. His love for music was ignited by his mother, who exposed him to a diverse range of artists spanning from Notorious B.I.G, Mary J. Blige and Sade to The Eagles, Bon Jovi and Israel Houghton in a single listening session. She is the reason why Moqemàe didn't know genres existed before he got into high school. Embracing this eclectic influence, Moqemàe identifies himself as a mood creator rather than being confined to a specific genre.
